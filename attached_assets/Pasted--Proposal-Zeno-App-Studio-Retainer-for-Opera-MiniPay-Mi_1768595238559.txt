Building on our successful collaboration in launching MiniPlay—which achieved nearly 150K users in under a month—we aim to drive MiniPay's growth by fostering innovation in mini apps that optimize for daily engagement, onchain transactions on Celo, and sustainable revenue models.

The proposed retainer is $25K per month, covering a dedicated team of three human agents, AI coding tools (e.g., Replit), servers, infrastructure, and operations. In alignment with Zeno's DAO LLC structure, we offer monthly $ZENO token allocations as "equity" incentives, vesting over time to share in the studio's compounding upside.

This partnership positions Zeno as MiniPay's go-to studio for co-building and improving apps, including those from third-party developers. We'll implement best practices derived from MiniPlay's launch and broader ecosystem insights, alongside a KPI framework to measure and optimize app performance. Ultimately, this collaboration will enhance MiniPay's user base (currently 12M+ activated wallets with 360M+ transactions), boost onchain activity on Celo, and contribute to Opera's overall value through creative, real-world Web3 applications.

## Background: Proven Success with MiniPlay

Over the past month, Zeno collaborated closely with the Opera MiniPay team to prototype, launch, and iterate on MiniPlay—the first mini app to gain significant traction on the platform. Key achievements include:
- Rapid user growth: Nearly 150K users acquired in less than one month, demonstrating strong product-market fit.
- High engagement: Users returning daily for interactive features, driving onchain transactions on Celo.
- Prototyping multiple follow-on apps: Ongoing discussions and builds for new mini apps, leveraging MiniPlay's momentum.

MiniPlay's success highlights the potential of MiniPay's ecosystem: a self-custodial stablecoin wallet integrated into the Opera Mini browser (and standalone apps on Android/iOS), with seamless access to third-party mini apps for savings, bill payments, vouchers, and more. Built on Celo's low-fee, mobile-first blockchain, MiniPay enables sub-cent transactions (~$0.001) and one-second settlements, making it ideal for everyday use in emerging markets across 60+ countries.

Lessons from MiniPlay inform our approach:
- **Community Management**: Open Telegram groups can dilute branding; channels with frequent updates and initiatives foster better engagement.
- **Scalability**: Apps must handle high traffic (hundreds of concurrent users) from day one.
- **Retention Mechanisms**: Daily check-ins tied to onchain transactions encourage habitual use.
- **Roadmap Discipline**: Launch with core features, then iterate regularly to maintain user excitement without overwhelming support.
- **Analytics and Admin Tools**: Integrate Google Analytics, custom dashboards, and admin panels for real-time insights and easy management.
- **Growth Tactics**: Referral systems and social sharing of progress amplify organic acquisition.

These insights, combined with Celo's developer resources (e.g., MiniPay templates, fee abstraction via viem/wagmi, and testnet faucets), position Zeno to deliver high-quality mini apps efficiently.

## Best Practices Guidelines for Mini Apps on MiniPay

Drawing from MiniPlay's launch, Celo documentation, and ecosystem best practices (e.g., mobile-first design, fee abstraction), Zeno recommends the following framework to optimize for daily engagement, onchain Celo transactions, and revenue:

### 1. **Design for Engagement and Retention**
   - Implement daily check-ins with onchain actions (e.g., simple Celo transactions for rewards like badges or progress tracking).
   - Avoid/limit direct user rewards; focus on intrinsic value (e.g., utility from stablecoin integrations).
   - Consider app coins selectively: For apps with network effects (e.g., social or gaming), introduce tokens for governance, access, or growth hacks—ensuring compliance and alignment with Celo's stablecoin focus.

### 2. **Community and Communication**
   - Use Telegram channels (not groups) for updates, polls, and initiatives to build hype without chaos.
   - Integrate feedback systems (e.g., in-app tickets) for quick iterations.

### 3. **Technical Scalability**
   - Build for high traffic: Use Celo's one-block finality and sub-cent fees; test with hundreds of simulated users.
   - Mobile-first: Ensure compatibility with MiniPay's embedded environment (Android/iOS/Opera Mini).
   - Fee Abstraction: Leverage viem/wagmi libraries for seamless transactions without gas management.

### 4. **Launch and Iteration Strategy**
   - MVP Approach: Start with core features; use a public roadmap for weekly/monthly updates to sustain excitement.
   - Avoid feature overload: Prioritize 2-3 key interactions per launch phase.

### 5. **Growth Mechanisms**
   - Referrals: Reward shares with onchain bonuses (e.g., bonus stablecoin access).
   - Social Integration: Enable easy sharing of achievements on X, WhatsApp, etc.

### 6. **Analytics and Admin**
   - Integrate Google Analytics for user behavior tracking.
   - Build internal dashboards (e.g., via Replit or cloud tools) for KPIs like DAU, transaction volume.
   - Admin Panels: For dynamic config (e.g., parameter tweaks without redeploys).

These guidelines ensure mini apps align with MiniPay's goal of turning stablecoins into everyday tools, driving real economic activity.

## KPI Framework for App Performance

To measure traction and guide decisions, Zeno proposes a standardized framework:
- **Core Metrics**:
  - Daily Active Users (DAU) and Retention Rate (e.g., 7-day/30-day).
  - Onchain Transactions: Volume and frequency per user on Celo.
  - Revenue: From in-app purchases, fees, or app coins (tracked via stablecoin flows).
  - Engagement: Session length, check-in completion rates.
- **Thresholds for Action**:
  - Green (Scale): DAU > 10K, Retention > 30%, Transactions > 1/user/day → Invest in features/marketing.
  - Yellow (Optimize): Below thresholds but positive trends → Iterate based on analytics.
  - Red (Delist): Stagnant/declining after 2-4 weeks → Sunset to free resources.
- **Tools**: Custom dashboards integrated with Celo explorers and Google Analytics; monthly reports to MiniPay team.

This data-driven approach ensures resources focus on winners, maximizing MiniPay's ecosystem health.
