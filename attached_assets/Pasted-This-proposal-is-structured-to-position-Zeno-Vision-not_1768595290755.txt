Proposal: Zeno Vision x Opera MiniPay
Accelerating Ecosystem Growth via an AI-Native Studio Partner
1. Executive Summary
Zeno Vision proposes a strategic partnership with Opera MiniPay to establish a dedicated MiniPay Studio Engine. Following the successful launch of MiniPlay (150k+ users in <30 days), Zeno Vision has proven its ability to ship high-traction, high-performance mini-apps rapidly.
We propose a monthly retainer model where Zeno acts as an extension of the MiniPay team. Our mandate is twofold: Build high-quality, engagement-driven apps internally, and Empower the wider ecosystem by establishing best practices and optimizing 3rd-party apps.
2. The Mandate: "Factory & Foundry"
Zeno will operate on two parallel tracks to drive value for MiniPay and the Celo ecosystem.
Track A: The Factory (Internal Builds)
Rapid Prototyping: We will prototype and launch new mini-apps monthly.
Focus: Engagement, Onchain Transactions (Celo), and Sustainable Revenue (moving away from "reward-farming" to genuine utility/fun).
App Coins: Strategic integration of app-specific tokens to drive retention and viral loops, not just speculation.
Track B: The Foundry (Ecosystem Optimization)
Developer Support: Assisting 3rd-party developers applying to list on MiniPay.
Audits & Refactoring: Reviewing promising apps for UX, onchain logic, and scalability before they get featured.
"Kill or Scale" Analysis: Using our data framework to recommend which apps deserve promotion and which should be delisted.
3. The "Zeno Standard": Best Practices Framework
Based on our real-world data from MiniPlay, we will enforce and disseminate the following "Zeno Standard" for successful MiniApps:
I. Community & Ops
Channels over Groups: Open Telegram groups dilute brand and increase FUD. We build announcement-heavy Channels for updates, reserving interactive support for dedicated ticket systems.
Scalability First: Apps must be architected for "Launch Day Spikes" (hundreds of concurrent users) using serverless/edge infrastructure to prevent crashing under MiniPay’s traffic power.
II. Product & Retention
Staged Roadmaps: Launch lean, then evolve. Releasing a complex app day one overwhelms users and kills the "new feature" excitement. We release features in waves to sustain retention.
Daily Onchain Habit: Every app must have a "Daily Check-in" mechanism that ideally triggers a low-cost onchain transaction on Celo, building user habit and network velocity.
III. Growth & Analytics
Social Proof Loops: Built-in referral systems that reward users for sharing progress/status on social media, not just inviting friends.
Data Sovereignty: Mandatory integration of Google Analytics + Internal Admin Dashboards. We don't fly blind; we measure engagement, churn, and revenue per user daily.
