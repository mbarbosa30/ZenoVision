4. Component B: The Routing Layer (ProsperON)
ProsperON is the native retention engine for Celo. It turns the unavoidable cost of using the network (gas) into a loyalty program for the entire chain.
The Mechanism:
 * Burn (Real Cost): User transactions burn CELO (deflationary).
 * Mint (Influence): Burning mints non-transferable Prosper Score.
 * Decay (Rent, Don't Own): Scores decay at 2% weekly. Influence is rented; you must remain active to maintain it.
 * Allocate (Yield): A weekly Yield Pool (stablecoins from endowment yield) is routed to users pro-rata based on their score.
Why this wins:
 * For Celo: It creates a counter-cyclical demand sink (Score is cheaper to earn when CELO price is low).
 * For Builders: "Use my app, earn Network Yield." Builders get a loyalty program out-of-the-box.
 * For Self: We bind Prosper Scores to Self IDs. This prevents wallet splitting (sybil attacks) from gaming the linear minting curve.

7. Critical Risk Assessment
| Risk | Mitigation Strategy |
|---|---|
| "Pay-to-Win" (Plutocracy) | 1. Decay: The 2% weekly decay means whales cannot buy influence once and sit on it; they must be active participants.
2. Linear Minting: There is no exponential bonus for massive burns.
3. Usage Bias: We reserve a portion of the pool for transaction frequency (usage), not just volume (burn). |

| "Black Mirror" (Privacy) | Degraded Mode Pattern. Users can always opt-out of sharing CHS signals. They sacrifice convenience/boosts, but never their fundamental right to transact. |
