Ok, after spending almost a month collaborating with Opera MiniPay team, and launching the first app that reached some good traction (MiniPlay with now almost 150K users in less than 1 month), and being discussing and prototyping more mini apps to be launched, I need to create a proposal as Zeno app studio (Zeno.vision) for a monthly retainer where the studio would build (and/or help build/improve apps from other developers also applying to list their app on MiniPay).  Im considering a budget of around $25K per month, including human agents (3 people), AI coding tools like Replit, servers and infrastructure and operations. As the Zeno studio will be a DAO LLC, it will have tokens as “equity”, which could be included in the proposal as a monthly reward for Opera MiniPay.


It is important that the studio has a kind of best practices guidelines to launch mini apps on MiniPay. The aim should be around optimizing for daily engagement, onchain transactions on Celo, and revenue (while avoiding or limiting mechanisms to reward users). The creation and integration of app coins (tokens to be used in the mini apps as a way to drive engagement and growth), could be considered depending on the mini app.  For context, here are some of the lessons from launching MiniPlay: - community management and engagement is very important, and having a good system for tickets or feedback is very important. Having an open telegram group where everyone can talk, can be damaging for the app branding and awareness, Better have a channel instead of a group, with frequent updates and initiatives. - the app should be prepared for high traffic since day one, like handling hundreds of users at the same time.
- Daily checkins mechanisms, ideally involving an unchain transaction, should be considered, as a way to incentivize users to come back to use the app regularly.
- Have a roadmap, so the app can launch or add or improve features on a regular basis, so users also feel the evolution and it becomes easier to manage its development and growth. Launching an app with a lot of features and options at the beginning will make it harder to support and can damage the ongoing effect of evolution and user excitement.
- Having google analytics integrated, and some internal dashboards to show relevant indicators, as well as an admin page to easily configure/change parameters and manage data, is important to consider.
- Also, important to consider referral mechanisms and incentivize users to share on social media their progress and enthusiasm.

It is also important to develop a framework that can measure main KPIs of the apps to understand its traction and where can be improved, or if ultimately it is not working and might be better to delist.
 Ultimately, the studio should built and structure to help drive and optimize MiniPay usage and growth, through innovation and creativity, that can also help influence Opera value.
